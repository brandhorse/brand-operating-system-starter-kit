# Approved Canon

> The frameworks the brand has committed to. Governed, versioned, and the things that don't change without a charter-level decision.

**Stage:** `articulation`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/canon-library

## What goes here

- `storytelling-frameworks/` — Approved narrative structures (e.g., problem→insight→shift).
- `communication-frameworks/` — How the brand structures messages by channel and stage.
- `brand-personas/` — The brand's own voice characters (founder, expert, mentor, etc.).

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
