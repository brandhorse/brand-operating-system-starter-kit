# Briefs & Templates

> Briefs anchor every campaign. Templates anchor every recurring artifact. Both inherit governance from the layers above.

**Stage:** `creation`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/briefs

## What goes here

- `campaign-briefs/` — Charter and member briefs for active campaigns.
- `asset-blueprints/` — Format manifests, slot schemas, blueprint examples.
- `story-arcs/` — Multi-asset narrative sequences (launches, nurture flows, evergreen pillars).

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
