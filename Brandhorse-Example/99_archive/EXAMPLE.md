# 99_archive — Brandhorse populated example

> Deprecated, retired, and superseded — kept, not deleted. The archive is a teaching tool, not a graveyard. If you can't say *why* something was archived, document it before moving it.

---

## deprecated-positioning/

**Archived: "Brand intelligence platform" framing (2024)**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Used | 2024-01 to 2024-09 |
| Why deprecated | "Brand intelligence" tested as too vague in customer interviews. People couldn't tell if we were a research tool, a dashboard, or an ops platform. |
| Replaced by | "Brand operating system" (current) |
| Decision log | DEC-2024-09-022 |
| Lesson | Category creation requires a concrete operational metaphor. "Operating system" works because it implies governance, processes, and audit. "Intelligence" is too soft. |

**Archived: "Brand single source of truth" (2023)**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Used | 2023-06 to 2024-01 |
| Why deprecated | Conflated us with asset-management tools (Frontify, Bynder). We're not a storage layer — we're a governance layer. |
| Replaced by | "Brand intelligence platform" (which itself was later replaced) |
| Decision log | DEC-2024-01-008 |
| Lesson | Don't position against a category you don't actually compete in. Storage was a non-goal. |

## retired-campaigns/

**Retired: "Brand audit week" (2025-Q3)**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Brief | BR-2025-08-AUDIT-WEEK |
| Status | Retired |
| Why | Time-boxed campaign concept underperformed; converted ~half the rate of the standing Brand Self-Audit funnel |
| Lesson | Time-boxed urgency works for product launches, not for evergreen lead magnets where the audit *is* the value |

**Retired: Email signature carousel (2025-Q4)**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Brief | BR-2025-11-EMAIL-SIG |
| Status | Retired |
| Why | Tried rotating a quote-of-the-week through team email signatures. Compliance overhead outweighed brand benefit. Most replies didn't notice. |
| Lesson | Brand surfaces with low recipient attention aren't worth the compliance burden. |

## How this folder feeds upstream

`07_approved-canon/` consults the archive when considering whether to revive or reference a deprecated framework. `02_patterns-and-insights/anti-patterns/` cites archived items to teach what to avoid. The archive is read-only — items are added when superseded but never removed.

**Rule for archiving:** Document the *why* in the same commit that moves an item. An undocumented archive is worse than a deleted file.
