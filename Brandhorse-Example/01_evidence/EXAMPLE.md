## competitive/

**Observation:** Major brand-strategy agencies (Pentagram, Wolff Olins, Landor) deliver positioning + visual identity decks. None of them ship the *operating system* underneath. The deliverable is the publication, not the process.

**Observation:** Notion, Frontify, and Bynder all sell "brand asset management" — but they're storage tools, not governance tools. They tell you where the file is, not whether it's allowed.

**Observation:** Nobody in this category positions against compliance. "Compliant" is treated as something legal handles, not something the brand operating system enforces.

**Implication:** Empty quadrant — governed brand operating system with compliance built in.
