## positioning/

**Category claim:**
Brandhorse is the **brand operating system** — the governed chain of command from evidence to execution that makes brand defensible at the system level, not the deliverable level.

**Frame of reference:**
For founders and operators of growing brands, when their brand decisions are scattered across docs, chats, and decks. *Unlike* brand-strategy agencies that deliver publications and *unlike* asset-management tools that store files without governance, Brandhorse turns positioning into a versioned operating system the whole team operates against.

**Differentiation:**
1. Compliance built in — rules fire as checks, not memos.
2. Audit trail by default — every decision is sourced and versioned.
3. The system, not the document — we ship the operating mode, not the publication.

**Provenance:** Insights 1, 2, 3 in `02_patterns-and-insights/`. Evidence rows: customer-quotes #1–4.
