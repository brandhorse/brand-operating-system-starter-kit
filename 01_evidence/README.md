# Evidence

> Raw signals — quotes, observations, competitive proof, market data. Where the chain of command starts.

**Stage:** `research`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/races/research/evidence

## What goes here

- `customer-quotes/` — Verbatims from interviews, calls, support tickets, reviews.
- `competitive/` — Competitor moves, positioning shifts, screenshots, ad libraries.
- `market-signals/` — Industry data, trend reports, search/SERP changes.
- `internal-proof/` — Sales calls, case-study source material, retention data.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
