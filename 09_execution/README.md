# Execution

> Shipped work. Where logos, fonts, colors, and templates live — explicitly downstream of governance, not above it.

**Stage:** `execution`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/assets

## What goes here

- `assets/` — Logos, fonts, colors, design tokens, photography, templates.
- `campaigns/` — Live and recently-shipped campaigns with their flights.
- `channel-shipped/` — Approved + published assets organized by channel.
- `compliance-runs/` — Audit trails — what passed, what failed, what was overridden.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
