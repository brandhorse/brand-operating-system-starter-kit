# 00_Start-Here

**Start here.** Before you upload a single logo, fill in the three documents in this folder:

1. **`Brand-Asset-Index.csv`** — the searchable index of every asset in the library. This is the file your team will open when "where's the logo" happens for the hundredth time.
2. **`Naming-Convention.txt`** — the pattern every file in the library should follow. Pick one and commit.
3. **`Contacts-and-Ownership.txt`** — who owns the brand, who approves new assets, who onboards new team members.

Every library has these three documents. Most brands skip them and pay for it later with chaos, duplicate assets, and the wrong blue on a homepage banner. Don't skip.

## What belongs in this folder

Documentation, not assets. Specifically:

- The three files above
- Any onboarding or training docs for new team members
- A change log of major library updates (optional — or use CHANGELOG.md at the repo root)
- Decision docs about brand choices that matter for asset creation (why we picked this blue, why we use this font for headlines)

## What does NOT belong

- Logos, fonts, photography, templates — those go in their respective top-level folders
- Sensitive or legal documents — those go in a private drive, not a public repo
