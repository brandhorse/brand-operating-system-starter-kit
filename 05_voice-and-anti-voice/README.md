# Voice & Anti-Voice

> How the brand speaks — and the things it can never say. Voice without anti-voice is only half the rule.

**Stage:** `articulation`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/voice

## What goes here

- `voice/` — Tone attributes, characteristic phrases, examples.
- `anti-voice/` — Words, phrases, tones, and patterns that violate the brand.
- `voice-overrides/` — Documented exceptions — when, why, and the rule that authorized them.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
