# Archive

Old approved assets. Don't delete — archive.

## Why archive instead of delete

- You'll need the old logo when you find an old deck with it embedded
- Brand evolution is a story; the archive documents the journey
- Legal sometimes needs to prove what was live on a given date
- The next rebrand references the previous one

## What goes here

- Logos that were approved but have been replaced
- Templates that have been superseded
- Old color palettes
- Previous brand guidelines documents
- Campaign-specific assets after the campaign ends

## What does NOT go here

- Drafts and working files (those live in `Working-Files/` subfolders of their category)
- Deleted files from team members' personal clutter (garbage is garbage, archive is documentation)

## Organization

Subfolder by category, then by year:

```
Archive/
├── Logos/
│   ├── 2024/
│   └── 2025/
├── Templates/
│   └── 2025/
└── Guidelines/
    └── 2024/
```

## Retention

Keep indefinitely for logos and guidelines. Review every 3 years for templates and campaign assets — keep what's referenced, purge what isn't.
