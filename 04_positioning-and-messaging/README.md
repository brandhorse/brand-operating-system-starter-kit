# Positioning & Messaging

> Governed positioning, message map, claims library. The strategic spine every brief inherits.

**Stage:** `articulation`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/messaging

## What goes here

- `positioning/` — Category claim, differentiation, frames of reference.
- `message-map/` — Pain → angle → message structure, persona-aware.
- `claims-library/` — Approved claims with evidence attribution and freshness dates.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
