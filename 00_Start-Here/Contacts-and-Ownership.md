## Onboarding

New team members should:

1. Read the full brand guidelines (`Guidelines/Full-Guide/`)
2. Scan `Messaging/Voice-and-Tone/`
3. Bookmark the asset index (`00_Start-Here/Brand-Asset-Index.csv`)
4. Know who the brand guardian is (above)
5. Ask before using an asset they can't find in the index
