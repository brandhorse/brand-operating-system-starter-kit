# Templates

Ready-to-use templates for the most common asset formats your team creates.

## Subfolder guide

- **`Documents/`** — proposals, one-pagers, case studies, letterhead, business cards.
- **`Email/`** — newsletter, announcement, transactional, drip campaigns (MJML, HTML, or platform-specific).
- **`Presentations/`** — master slide decks (Google Slides, Keynote, PowerPoint, Pitch).
- **`Signatures/`** — email signature HTML and image variants.
- **`Social-Media/`** — feed posts, stories, ads, cover images (Figma, Canva, PSD).

## What makes a good template

- **Brand elements locked** — logo, colors, typography
- **Content fields open** — text, images, CTA
- **Mobile-responsive** — especially email and social
- **Multiple variations** — a straitjacket template won't get used. Give options.
- **Versioned** — bump the version when you update; archive the old one

## What NOT to put here

- Finished published assets (those belong in a CMS or `Archive/` if worth keeping)
- Client-specific deliverables (if this fork is public)

## Naming

```
brand-template-[category]-[name]-v[n].ext
```
