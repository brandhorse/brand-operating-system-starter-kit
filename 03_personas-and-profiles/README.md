# Personas & Profiles

> Who you serve, who you don't, who you used to. Audience definition precedes positioning, messaging, and voice.

**Stage:** `articulation`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/races/research/customer-profiles

## What goes here

- `icp/` — The primary buyer / user the brand is built for.
- `secondary-personas/` — Adjacent buyers / users worth serving but not optimizing for.
- `anti-personas/` — Who the brand explicitly does NOT serve. Saying no is a positioning act.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
