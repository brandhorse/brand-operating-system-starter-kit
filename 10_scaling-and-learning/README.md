# Scaling & Learning

> Experiments, performance reviews, and the decisions log. The feedback loop that keeps the OS honest.

**Stage:** `scaling`  ·  **OS surface:** /app/races/scaling

## What goes here

- `experiments/` — Test hypotheses, results, and the decisions they unlocked.
- `performance/` — Per-asset and per-campaign metrics, surfaced against the brief's KPI.
- `decisions-log/` — What changed, why, and which evidence row anchored the change.
- `recaps/` — Quarterly / launch / experiment retrospectives.

## What doesn't go here

If you're tempted to put something in this folder that doesn't fit any of the
subfolders above, ask whether the artifact actually belongs upstream or
downstream. Most "where does this go?" moments come from skipping a step in
the chain of command.
